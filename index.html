LUMINHIVE
A COZY BEE WORLD · A DANGEROUS HONEY ECONOMY
① CLAIM A HIVE

You spawn in the apiary plaza facing a row of hives. Walk to any vacant one and press E to claim it — the rest get taken by rival keepers.

② WATCH IT HATCH

Your first bee crawls out of its honeycomb cell. Bees live in the comb and pass through it to reach their slots.

③ HARVEST & REFINE

Hold the left mouse button near flowers, chase ability tokens, fill the pack, then return home to bank pollen as honey.

④ GROW & UNLOCK

Buy eggs, hatch bees into hex slots, unlock stronger fields by bee count, then use extra honey in the market.
